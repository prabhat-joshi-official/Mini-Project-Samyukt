OUR CRAFT

From Flour To Loaf

We believe exceptional bread cannot be rushed. Every loaf begins with carefully sourced organic ingredients and a naturally cultivated starter.

Through a meticulous 36-hour fermentation process, our dough develops rich flavor, a delicate texture, and the character that defines true artisan bread.

Each morning before sunrise, our bakers hand-shape every loaf and bake it fresh in traditional stone-deck ovens, creating a crisp golden crust and a soft, flavorful crumb.
